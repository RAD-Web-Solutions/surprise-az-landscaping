Why Should I Use Artificial Turf?

There are numerous advantages to using artificial turf over natural grass, including:


No more maintenance: There is no need to mow the lawn anymore. Artificial turf does not require watering, mowing, weeding, dethatching, fertilization, or aeration. Weeds and pesticides will become obsolete. The ultimate in low-maintenance landscaping is artificial grass.


No more watering: Droughts occur almost every year in Arizona, and adhering to watering restrictions can be difficult. However, replacing your lawn with artificial grass eliminates the need for a watering schedule and sprinklers.


Prevent event/heavy traffic damage: An evening of entertaining a large group in your backyard can leave the grass in shambles. The situation is even worse for event properties.


Prevent damage from dog: Artificial turf resists damage from dogs and cats by repelling them. Artificial turf is also great at keeping your dog entertained and can be used as a protective barrier to keep your dog safe and provide a safe playing area for your dog. Artificial turf is the perfect surface for training your dog to exercise on, and it can also be a place for your dog to play and relax when he or she doesn’t want to go outside or go for a walk.


Reduce your environmental impact: Because artificial grass does not require watering, it helps you conserve water and save money. If sustainability is important to you, we can also assist you in selecting an environmentally friendly turf made from recycled materials.


Achieve natural-looking beauty: The days of cheap-looking artificial turf are over. There are a variety of artificial grass colors and textures available, and all of them will remain green all year. Many of our customers prefer the perfect appearance of synthetic grass to typical monoculture live grass lawns.


Make playgrounds safer for children: Some artificial turfs have excellent fall ratings, which help protect children from the full impact of trips and drops from the monkey bars. Furthermore, no matter how much they are played on, they will not turn brown.


Enjoy a playing field: From golf to bocce ball, some of our favorite sports require dependable grass in order to be played. Artificial lawns are ideal for sports turf, and we can even assist you in selecting a blend that is specifically designed for your preferred sport.
